Here's Exactly How It Works

No confusion. No surprises. Just a clear path from first contact to final delivery.

1

You Reach Out

What Happens:

You send a message:

  • DM on Instagram
  • Email: monnybethwel@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +254758434092

I Respond (Within 4 Hours):

I ask a few quick questions to understand your situation.

2

Quick Chat (15 Minutes)

What Happens:

We hop on a brief call (or message exchange if you prefer).

I Ask:

  • What's prompting you to seek clarity now?
  • What's your biggest challenge?
  • What would success look like?
  • What's your timeline?

Purpose:

Make sure we're a good fit. Not everyone needs this, and that's okay.

3

You Book & Pay

What Happens:

If we're aligned, I send you:

  • Service details
  • Pricing options
  • Invoice

You:

  • Choose your tier
  • Pay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or PayPal
  • Receive confirmation

Payment secured = Your session is booked.

4

You Prepare (Takes 30-45 Minutes)

What Happens:

I send you a simple form with questions about your brand:

  • Your story and background
  • Who you serve
  • What you offer
  • Current challenges
  • Goals and vision

Why This Matters:

Your answers help me prepare strategic questions specifically for you.

Deadline: Complete within 3 days of booking.

5

I Prepare (Behind the Scenes)

What I Do:

  • Review your intake form deeply
  • Research your industry and competitors
  • Audit your current online presence
  • Prepare strategic questions
  • Create session outline

You don't see this work, but it's crucial.

6

The Strategy Session (3-4 Hours)

What Happens:

Format: Video call (Zoom or Google Meet)
Recorded: Yes (for your reference)

Hour 1 - Discovery:

We explore your story, vision, and what makes you unique.

Hour 2 - Definition:

We craft your positioning statement and messaging framework.

Hour 3 - Direction:

We map visual direction, content themes, and communication strategy.

Hour 4 - Implementation:

We build your 60-day roadmap and prioritize quick wins.

Your Role:

  • Show up ready to think
  • Answer honestly
  • Make decisions
  • Trust the process
7

You Get Your Blueprint (3-5 Days Later)

What You Receive:

A professional PDF document (15-25 pages) with:

  • Your brand positioning (clear and simple)
  • Messaging framework (exact words to use)
  • Audience definition (who and why)
  • Visual direction (colors, fonts, style)
  • 60-day action plan (what to do first)
  • Resources and next steps

This becomes your brand bible.
Reference it. Use it. Build from it.

8

Follow-Up Call (1-2 Weeks Later)

What Happens:

We meet for 1 hour to:

  • Answer any questions
  • Review your implementation progress
  • Refine anything that needs adjustment
  • Ensure you're confident moving forward

After this, you're fully equipped to implement.

Total Timeline

Week 1
Booking, intake, prep
Week 2
Strategy session
Week 3
Blueprint delivery + follow-up call

2-3 weeks from start to finish.

What Happens After?

Most Clients:

  • Update all social profiles with new messaging
  • Implement quick wins from the roadmap
  • Hire designer for visual execution (I can recommend)
  • Launch content using new clarity
  • See results within 30 days

Some Clients:

  • Book monthly support for ongoing guidance
  • Return when launching new initiatives
  • Refer others who need clarity
